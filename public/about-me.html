About Me

Hello, my name is Eric Rodriguez and I was born in the vibrant and culturally rich country of Cuba, where I spent the first 14 years of my life. In 2018, my family and I embarked on a new journey, moving to the United States. This transition marked a significant change in my life, bringing new challenges and opportunities. Adjusting to a new country, learning a new language, and making new friends have all been part of my incredible journey of growth and self-discovery.

After arriving in the US, I dedicated myself to my education, culminating in my high school graduation. Currently, I am pursuing a degree in computer science, a field that fascinates me with its endless possibilities for innovation and problem-solving. The ever-evolving nature of technology excites me, and I am eager to contribute to this dynamic field. My studies have not only enhanced my technical skills but also deepened my understanding of how technology can improve lives and drive change.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I enjoy staying active and spending quality time with my loved ones. Going to the gym is one of my favorite activities, providing a perfect balance to my studies and helping me maintain a healthy lifestyle. Whether it's lifting weights, running, or trying out new workouts, the gym is a place where I can challenge myself and unwind. Additionally, I cherish moments spent with family and friends, as they bring joy, support, and inspiration into my life. Balancing my passions and commitments is important to me, and I am grateful for the experiences and people that shape who I am today.
